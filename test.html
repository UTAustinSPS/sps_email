September 5, 2013

New items are BLUE. Last chance items are RED.

Weekly Meeting

  1. Intro to SPS

    September 5th, 6pm, RLM 11.204

    Come hear what UT SPS is all about! We'll be talking about what different activities we're planning and would love for the meeting to be as interactive as possible!

  2. Physics Careers

    September 12th, 6pm, CPE 2.208

    Dr. Franks from the Career Design Center will join us to talk about careers in physics and what resources are available on campus for finding jobs! We'll also unveil a project we'll be working on with the Career Design Center over the course of the semester. If time permits, SPS President Evan Ott will present his research from this summer at Sandia National Laboratories.

Social Events

  1. Physnic

    September 6th, 5pm, Eastwoods Park

    This Friday is the Physnic (Physics Department Picnic)! Come to Eastwoods Park just Northeast of RLM at 5pm and get some delicious BBQ! You might even play some Ultimate Frisbee with your professors! It's a great time for the Department of Physics and a great way to meet your fellow physicists!
